FATAL RECORDS

1. Gary Kotlyar
2. Sam Feliciano
Fatal Records is based in Oakland, CA, and was founded in 2019 by childhod best friends Gary Kotlyar and Sam Feliciano.

Gary Kotlyar

Raised amongst the misty embers at the foothills of The Santa Cruz mountains in Cupertino, Gary Kotlyar picked up the six string axe at the tender age of 11. Shaped and bolstered by a community of local strummers, bangers and dreamers, Gary hit the clubs of the Bay Area with a number of heavy metal and thrash metal outfits, cut his teeth at studios around the Bay Area, all while studying jazz piano and music theory with Juilliard ducated pianist Susan Jackson. In his early 20s, Kotlyar dipped his toe in Hollywood’s pool, composing his first score for the award winning documentary feature The Rugby Player, which was broadcast nationally on PBS and toured festivals all around the world. Around this time, Kotlyar traversed America supporting legendary Blues man Chris Cotton for Fat Possum records. Coming off the success of the film, his first full length album and tour, Kotlyar decided to form Fatal Records to release his wide variety of music projects and film score soundtracks. The rest, as they say, is history!

Sam Feliciano

Born into the famed musical Feliciano family, Sam was trained by his Grammy award-nominated father Ric Feliciano in music and composition from a very early age. As he grew, Sam was taken under the wing of famed businessman and inventor Edgar Sacks, picking up the social skills that have helped create the wide network of collaborators Sam and Gary work with today. While studying, teaching and playing music after high school, Sam apprenticed as a journeyman plumber and businessman, learning the art of the deal at various plumbing companies around the Bay Area, in addition to mastering music production alongside Gary Kotlyar. Feliciano’s penchant for making a good deal, his keen sense for sweet tones, and a shared love of making records lead him to collaborate with best friend Kotlyar to form Fatal Records in 2019. His duties at Fatal Records include music production, making deals, video production and the general nitty-gritty of running a record label. Feliciano is a dog lover and a tireless curator of music playlists
